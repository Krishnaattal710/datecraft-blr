Got questions?
Everything you
need to know
How far in advance should I book?
+
We recommend booking at least 3–5 days before the date to ensure venue availability and gift sourcing. For the Fairytale plan (full-day + décor), 7–10 days is ideal. Last-minute bookings (under 48 hours) are sometimes possible — WhatsApp us directly.
Do I pay the full amount upfront?
+
We take a 50% advance payment to confirm your booking. The remaining 50% is due 24 hours before the date. If we can't deliver for any reason, you get a full refund — no questions asked.
Can I customise what's in the plan?
+
Absolutely. Every plan is a starting point. You can swap the café, add a specific gift, request a particular activity, or modify the timeline. Just mention it in the special notes field and we'll accommodate it wherever possible.
Does my partner know it's planned by DateCraft?
+
Only if you want them to. We operate completely behind the scenes. The table card, the gifts, the timeline — everything looks like you planned it yourself. We'll even coach you on what to say if they ask how you pulled it off. 😄
What if my partner doesn't like the venue?
+
You approve the venue before we confirm it. We'll send you 2–3 shortlisted options based on your preferences, you pick one, we book it. Nothing is finalised without your sign-off.
Do you operate outside Bengaluru?
+
Currently we're Bengaluru-only — covering Koramangala, Indiranagar, HSR, Whitefield, Jayanagar, Sadashivanagar, JP Nagar, and surrounding areas. Mumbai and Pune launches are planned for Q3 2025.
